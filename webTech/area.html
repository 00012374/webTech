The Area

Vesterborg, Denmark

Vesterborg

The treetop hotel is situated in a small forest by Als Odde, a rural destination in the northern part of Denmark close to both the sea and Mariager Fjord. It is a peaceful and beautiful area without mass tourism, and perfect for biking, hiking and fishing.
